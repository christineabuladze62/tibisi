PROJECTS

One of the most memorable projects I've worked on is the Moon Camp Challenge, organized in collaboration with ESA and NASA. As a team, we designed a 3D model of a lunar base, thinking about how humans might live and work on the Moon. It was both challenging and exciting, and it taught me how to collaborate and solve real-world problems creatively.

Through various 3D modeling projects, I have created detailed digital designs ranging from architectural models to creative concepts for competitions. One notable project involved designing a futuristic building, where I applied technical skills and artistic vision to bring the idea to life. These projects have strengthened my ability to plan, problem-solve, and communicate visually.

In the literary club, I participated in several projects including organizing reading sessions, writing short stories, and hosting debates on classic and modern literature. One key project was a collaborative anthology where club members contributed original poems and essays, which helped me improve my writing and teamwork skills while deepening my love for literature.
